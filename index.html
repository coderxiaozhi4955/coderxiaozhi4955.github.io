XPath Locator Privacy Policy Test

1. Introduction

XPath Locator ("we" or "our Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information Collection

Our Extension only collects the following information:

  • Temporary access to DOM elements of the current webpage when you use the element locating feature
  • Local storage of your XPath and CSS selector search history

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Browsing history
  • Login credentials
  • Passwords or other sensitive data

We do NOT transmit any data to external servers

3. Use of Information

The collected information is used solely for:

  • Implementing webpage element location and property retrieval features
  • Providing search history functionality
  • Improving user experience

4. Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally in your browser only
  • Search history is stored using Chrome browser's local storage functionality
  • You can clear your history at any time

5. Third-Party Access

Our Extension does not share your information with any third parties.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • View your search history
  • Delete individual history entries
  • Clear all history
  • Uninstall the extension and remove all associated data

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on the Chrome Web Store extension page.
